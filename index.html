🍜 Thai Pork && Noodles Recipe 🍜
  • 1 pound thin boneless pork chops, fat trimmed
  • 8 ounces wide Thai rice noodles
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro (leaves and stems)
  • Finely grated zest && juice of 1 lime
  • 2 slices peeled ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 3 chopped jalapeno peppers
  • 2 shallots, roughly chopped
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 4 tablespoons peanut oil


  • 1/4 pound green beans, split lengthwise and cut into pieces

Description : -

  1. Place the pork in the freezer about 10 minutes to make it easier to slice.

  2. Soak the noodles in very hot water until just tender, about 10 minutes; drain.

  3. Snip the noodles with scissors into shorter lengths.

  4. Cut the frozen pork crosswise into 1/4-inch-wide strips.

  5. Puree the cilantro, lime zest and juice, ginger, garlic, jalapenos, shallots, fish sauce, brown sugar and 1 tablespoon water in a food processor until smooth, scraping down the processor as needed.

  6. Heat 3 tablespoons peanut oil in a large skillet over high heat.

  7. Add half the cilantro mixture and stir-fry 1 minute.

  8. Add the pork and stir-fry until no longer pink, 2 to 3 minutes.

  9. Transfer the pork with a slotted spoon to a plate.

  10. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon peanut oil to the skillet; add the green beans and stir-fry until tender, 4 to 5 minutes.

  11. Add the noodles, pork and 2 tablespoons of the remaining cilantro mixture.

  12. Cook, tossing with tongs, until heated through, about 2 minutes.

  13. Stir in the remaining cilantro mixture to taste.
